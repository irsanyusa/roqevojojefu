    What is architecture?

    Architecture is a method of technology in building connected with the design of construction. For today, it`s one of the greatest application of art and building. Therefore, you will need to study several categories of building engineering. If you need to apply for the Architecture and Building technology, then you need the follow JAMB subject combination for the architect:

    • English;
    • Mathematics;
    • Physics;
    • Chemistry/Economics/Biology/Art/Geography.

    If you want to be admitted as an aspirant. Then you are required to take the following subjects that are required for WAEC:

    • Book Keeping;
    • Computer Studies;
    • Agricultural Sciences;
    • Biology;
    • Physics;
    • Chemistry;
    • Mathematics;
    • English Language.

    • University of Lagos – Geography, Economics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics and Mathematics.
    • Abia State University – Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

    • Caleb University – Technical Drawing, Fine Art, Chemistry, Biology, Geography, Economics, Physics, Mathematics and English Language.
