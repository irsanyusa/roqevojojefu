    Makeup Artist Transforms Lady's Face With Black Patches, Looks Incredible: &quot;Cover Face Like Lagbaja&quot;

    Social media users could not contain their reactions as a makeup artist flaunted how she transformed one of her clients' faces. In a video, the makeup artist (@raynaglam123 on Instagram) showed the face of a lady with an obvious black patch covering several areas.

    25 May 2024, 00:00
    • A makeup artist made a stunning impression in the minds of netizens after she shared one of her recent works
    • She transformed the face of her client, who had black patches, and showed off the process she followed to achieve her result
    • Some social media users argued that the makeup artist used a filter and it was not possible to attain such total transformation on her client

    Social media users could not contain their reactions as a makeup artist flaunted how she transformed one of her clients' faces.

    In a video, the makeup artist (@raynaglam123 on Instagram) showed the face of a lady with an obvious black patch covering several areas.

    She began work as she applied a liquid substance to clean up her face before adding different shades of powder.
