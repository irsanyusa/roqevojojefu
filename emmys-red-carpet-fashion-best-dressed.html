    Emmys 2021 Red Carpet Arrivals (Photos)

    25 Jul 2024, 00:00

    This year’s Emmy Awards mark the return to an in-person ceremony for the star-studded event, making walking the red carpet all the more special for the nominees and other Hollywood guests.

    Accordingly, television stars arrived to L.A. Live’s Event Deck dressed to the nines, donning gorgeous gowns and dapper tuxes. The trend of the night was certainly color, as both Jason Sudeikis and Bo Burnham showed up in velvet blue suits, Kaley Cuoco rocked a neon green dress and Nicole Byer stunned in a bright purple off-the-shoulder gown.

    Accessories also owned the carpet, with “The Crown” star Josh O’Connor sporting a statement flower-shaped bow tie on the carpet. Meanwhile, “SNL” actor Bowen Yang pumped up his suit with some silver platform heels.

    See all of the fashion from the 2021 Emmys below.

    • Elizabeth Olsen

      Elizabeth Olsen wore a cream-colored dress with billowing sleeves designed by The Row, the brand founded by her sisters Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen. Olsen is nominated for lead actress in a limited series for her role in “WandaVision.”

    • Jason Sudeikis

      Jason Sudeikis stepped out in a blue velvet suit by Tom Ford on the Emmys red carpet. Sudeikis is up for four awards tonight for “Ted Lasso,” including lead actor in a comedy series.

    • Rosie Perez

      Rosie Perez shimmers in an intricate gold gown on the Emmys red carpet. Perez is up for supporting actress in a comedy series for “The Flight Attendant.”

    • Billy Porter

      Billy Porter rocks a black jumpsuit with mega-ruffled sleeves. Porter is nominated for outstanding lead actor in a drama series for “Pose.” 

    • Bowen Yang

      Bowen Yang arrives on the Emmys red carpet wearing a classic suit and killer silver platforms to match his Tiffany & Co jewelry. Yang is nominated for supporting actor in a comedy series for his work as a featured player on “SNL.”

    • Robin Thede

      Robin Thede dazzled in a sea foam green gown on the Emmys red carpet. Thede is up for two Emmys tonight for her work on “A Black Lady Sketch Show.”

    • Josh O’Connor

      “The Crown” star Josh O’Connor sported a black tie with a flower embellishment on the Emmys red carpet.

    • Nicole Byer

      “Nailed It” host Nicole Byer stunned in a bright purple off-the-shoulder dress with ruffle detailing.

    • Kaley Cuoco

      Kaley Cuoco is a lime green dream in an open-front gown with matching heels. Cuoco is nominated for lead actress in a comedy series for her starring role in “The Flight Attendant.”

    • Kenan Thompson

      Kenan Thompson looks dapper as can be in an all-pink ensemble, complete with some aviator sunglasses. Thompson is up for both the lead and supporting actor in a comedy series awards for his work on “Kenan” and “SNL,” respectively. 

    • Anthony Anderson

      “Black-ish” star Anthony Anderson stepped out in a champagne-colored jacket with a black floral pattern complete with an Omega watch. Anderson is vying for lead actor in a comedy series for “Black-ish,” and the show is also nominated for outstanding comedy series.

    • Hannah Einbinder

      “Hacks” star Hannah Einbinder wore a sleeveless pink gown complete with a tufted train. Einbinder is up for supporting actress in a comedy series for portraying Ava Daniels on “Hacks.”

    • Karamo Brown

      Karamo Brown of “Queer Eye” wore a velvet tux with a matching black bow tie.

    • Carl Clemons-Hopkins

      “Hacks” actor Carl Clemons-Hopkins made a statement on the Emmys red carpet with an off-the-shoulder white top complete with a colorful cummerbund. Clemons-Hopkins is nominated for supporting actor in a comedy series for his work in “Hacks.” 

    • Yara Shahidi

      “Black-ish” star Yara Shahidi looked gorgeous in a green gown complete with gold accessories. 

    • Jac Schaeffer

      “WandaVision” showrunner Jac Schaeffer wore a dress covered in red flowers on the Emmys red carpet.

    • Cynthia Erivo

      Cynthia Erivo strutted onto the carpet in a white dress with blue and green feathers at the bottom by Louis Vuitton. Erivo is nominated for outstanding lead actress in a limited or anthology series for portraying Aretha Franklin in “Genius: Aretha.” 

    • Jennifer Coolidge

      Jennifer Coolidge was stunning in navy blue on the Emmys red carpet. “The White Lotus” star is a presenter during tonight’s ceremony.

    • Kate Winslet

      Kate Winslet kept it classic in a black gown with sheer sleeves. Winslet is nominated for lead actress in a limited series for her role on “Mare of Easttown.”

    • Rachel Lindsay

      “Extra” host Rachel Lindsay sported a clementine-colored gown to the Emmys.

    • Zuri Hall and Scott Evans

      Access Hollywood host Zuri Hall and Scott Evans arrived on the red carpet in an off-white gown and brown suit.

    • Taraji P. Henson

      Taraji P. Henson rocked a black-and-white splattered gown with a plunging neckline on the Emmys red carpet.

    • Brendan Hunt and Shannon Nelson

      “Ted Lasso” actor and writer Brendan Hunt and actor Shannon Nelson arrive to the Emmys red carpet. Hunt is nominated for four Emmys tonight for his role as Coach Beard on the show as well as writing. 

    • Josh Heald, Jon Hurwitz and Hayden Schlossberg

      “Cobra Kai” creators Josh Heald, Jon Hurwitz and Hayden Schlossberg all rocked classic black tuxes.

    • Cat M. Sullivan, Patrick Doody and Shea Spencer

      “Nailed It” executive producers Cat M. Sullivan, Patrick Doody and Shea Spencer arrived to the 2021 Emmys red carpet.

    • Nischelle Turner and Kevin Frazier

      “Entertainment Tonight” hosts Nischelle Turner and Kevin Frazier arrived to the 2021 Emmys red carpet.

    • ‘The Amber Ruffin Show’ Writers and Cast

      “The Amber Ruffin Show” cast members and writers arrive to the 2021 Emmys red carpet. 

    • Cedric the Entertainer

      The show’s host with the most, Cedric the Entertainer, was a vision in Tiffany blue on the red carpet. 

    • Amber Ruffin

      Amber Ruffin wears a blue one-shoulder gown with an intricate flower design on the Emmys red carpet. She is nominated for outstanding writing for a variety series for “The Amber Ruffin Show.” 

    • Ashley Nicole Black

      Ashley Nicole Black wears a pink and orange color-blocked dress by Burnett New York with jewelry from Natalie Mills and Lang Antiques. She is nominated for outstanding writing for a variety series for both “The Amber Ruffin Show” and “A Black Lady Sketch Show.”

    • Rita Wilson

      Rita Wilson arrives to the Emmys red carpet in a sleek all-black Tom Ford ensemble accompanied by silver jewelry. 

    • Ariel Dumas

      Ariel Dumas, head writer for “The Late Show With Stephen Colbert,” rocks a pink and red dress with blue accessories. Dumas is up for two Emmys for her work on “The Late Show.”

    • Chris Sullivan and Rachel Sullivan

      “This Is Us” actor Chris Sullivan and Rachel Sullivan sported contrasting printed suits on the Emmys red carpet. 

    • John Oliver

      John Oliver opted for a classic tux at this year’s Emmy Awards. “Last Week Tonight” is nominated for two awards tonight.

    • Kathryn Hahn

      Kathryn Hahn looked absolutely bewitching in a two-piece black ensemble with a statement belt. Hahn is nominated for supporting actress in a limited series for her performance as Agatha in “WandaVision.”

    • Samira Wiley

      Samira Wiley roced a sleek black suit with gold detailing on the Emmys red carpet. Wiley is up for supporting actress in a drama series for playing Moira on “The Handmaid’s Tale.”

    • Beth Behrs

    • Daniel Dae Kim

      Daniel Dae Kim paired a classic tux with square-framed glasses for the Emmys red carpet. 

    • Michael Douglas and Catherine Zeta-Jones

      Michael Douglas and Catherine Zeta-Jones arrived on the Emmys red carpet hand-in-hand. Douglas wore a subtly-striped suit while Zeta-Jones stunned in a deep-red strapless dress.

    • GaTa and Lil Dicky

      “Dave” stars GaTa and Lil Dicky arrive on the Emmys red carpet in contrasting suits.

    • Trevor Noah

      “The Daily Show” host Trevor Noah arrived to the Emmys red carpet in a classic black suit and bowtie. Noah is nominated for outstanding variety talk series, as well as variety special for hosting this year’s Grammy Awards. 

    • Ann Dowd

      Ann Dowd opted for a black dress with sheer sleeves. She is nominated for supporting actress in a drama series for “The Handmaid’s Tale.” 

    • Annaleigh Ashford

      Annaleigh Ashford paired a white strapless dress with a red lip on the Emmys red carpet. 

    • Madeline Brewer

      Madeline Brewer arrived to the Emmys red carpet in a bronze dress by Tom Ford and blue heels. Brewer is nominated for supporting actress in a drama series for her role as Janine on “The Handmaid’s Tale.”

    • Akilah Green

      Akilah Green stuns in a blue gown paired with a pink lip. Green is nominated for oustanding writing for a variety series for her work on “A Black Lady Sketch Show.”

    • Angela Bassett

      Angela Bassett makes a statement in a black gown with hot pink ruffle detailing by Greta Constantine, complete with Gismondi 1754 jewelry. 

    • Dan Levy

      “Schitt’s Creek” star Dan Levy stunned in an all-blue ensemble on the Emmys red carpet. Levy is nominated for guest actor in a comedy series for hosting “Saturday Night Live.”

    • Eugene Levy

      Eugene Levy wore a black suit with a white bowtie and red boutonnière. 

    • Jean Smart

      Jean Smart looked dazzling in a black gown with a sheer high neck by Ralph Lauren. Smart is nominated for both lead actress in a comedy series and supporting actress in a limited series for “Hacks” and “Mare of Easttown,” respectively. 

    • Jessica Long

      Jessica Long wore a black-and-gold dress to the Emmys red carpet, pairing it with the perfect accessory — her gold medal from the Tokyo Olympics.

    • Mindy Kaling

    • Tracee Ellis Ross

      Annie Murphy wore a teal gown with a slit in the front on the Emmys red carpet.

      Conan O’Brien rocked a sleek black suit on the Emmys red carpet. “Conan” is nominated for outstanding variety talk series.

    • Eugene Levy and Dan Levy
