    Jamie Lee Curtis Left the 2024 Oscars Early, but the Reason Is Super Relatable

    Jamie Lee Curtis remains one ofthe most down-to-earth celebrities! The 65-year-old Oscar winner was present atSundays 96th annual Academy Awards, though she left the event early for arelatable reason.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Jamie Lee Curtis remains one of the most down-to-earth celebrities! The 65-year-old Oscar winner was present at Sunday’s 96th annual Academy Awards, though she left the event early for a relatable reason.

    DAVID SWANSON/AFP/East News

    I, so love, her

    In the early part of the evening, Curtis graced the 2024 Oscars stage to present the award for Best Supporting Actress to the deserving winner, Da’Vine Joy Randolph. She shared the stage with several other past winners in the category, such as Mary Steenburgen, Lupita Nyong’o, Rita Moreno, and Regina King.

    However, soon after completing her duty, the 65-year-old seemingly departed from the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood and opted for a post-show fast-food dinner at In-N-Out.

    Okay, how come it looks like guacamole dip all over her beautiful face???

    In a post on Instagram, she wrote, «FLY IN ✅ GET FLUFFED AND FOLDED ✅ PRESENT AT OSCARS ✅ GO TO @inandout_burger ✅ FLY AWAY,» alongside photos of her enjoying a burger, fries, and soda at the beloved Southern California drive-through restaurant.

    Despite her brief appearance at this year’s Oscars, where she made a quick stop (pun intended), the 65-year-old still managed to turn heads on the red carpet with a striking long-sleeved black gown by Dolce and Gabbana.

    DAVID SWANSON/AFP/East News

    Jamie Lee Curtis and Arnold Schwarzenegger recently reunited nearly 30 years after their iconic movie True Lies was released. Jamie Lee Curtis shared adorable photos of the duo on her Instagram, evoking a wave of nostalgia.
