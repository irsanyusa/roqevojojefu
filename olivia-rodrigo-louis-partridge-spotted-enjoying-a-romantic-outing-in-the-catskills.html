    Olivia Rodrigo and Louis Partridge are not afraid to show off their new romance. After being spotted kissing in New York City, the pop princess and actor were seen strolling around the old-fashioned streets of Tannersville.

    Olivia Rodrigo, Louis Partridge Spotted Having a Date in The Catskills

    On December 14, Rodrigo and Partridge were seen leisurely walking the quaint Catskills town of Tannersville. An eyewitness told People that it was the “Vampire” singer and the Enola Holmes star’s first time in the said place.

    “They were browsing the apothecary goods at a shop called Sundry on Main Street,” an onlooker stated. “They were very cute and [were] overheard saying it was their first time in the area; they mostly spoke with gazes and body language. It was very cute.”

    Near Hunter Mountain, Tannersville is about two and a half hours away from New York City. Over the years, the location has been a perfect spot for celebrities who want to unwind from the spotlight.

    Back in 2019, Selena Gomez shared a photo of herself enjoying food at the town’s Selena’s Diner. There, she offered to refill customer’s coffee cups and laughed after she realized she wasn’t recognized.

    The outing comes after Rodrigo and Partridge were spotted kissing at a gas station in New York City. At one point, the actor lifted the “Driver’s License” hitmaker while sharing a passionate kiss with her.

    At the time, the pop princess was donning a fur-trimmed beige coat and a pair of dark brown corduroy trousers. She was also wearing a pair of brown leather boots and small black shades.

    Appearing to spot the paparazzo, the romantic duo opened the trunk of their car and began laughing hysterically while posing for selfies. It was during this time that Rodrigo and Partridge seemingly confirmed their romance.

    They Are Reportedly “Inseparable” From Each Other

    In late October, a source revealed that the singer went to UK to see the young heartthrob. Rodrigo flew to London with her best friend Conan Gray, but it was Partridge who she came to see.

    “They met through mutual pals earlier in the year and have been messaging quite a bit,” the insider said. “She has spent a few days in the capital hanging out with them all but it’s been Louis who she has been inseparable from.”

    Partridge, 20, is the same age as Rodrigo. Since they met, they had been together for dinners and nights out. During their time in London, they hit a club together and “were acting very coupley.”

    “It’s really sweet to see her happy,” the insider said.

    Before her relationship with Partridge, Rodrigo dated actor and singer Joshua Bassett in 2020. Later, she sparked romance with producer Adam Faze and music executive Zack Bia.
