How to use 'se' in Spanish

30 Jun 2024, 00:00

The word se in Spanish has a variety of uses. It is part of reflexive verbs, an indirect object pronoun and an imperative. The different uses of se can be easy to confuse, especially when listening.  Spanish-language learners start learning about se as beginners, when se appears as a pronoun in reflexive verbs. These are verbs that always go together with a pronoun when an action is being performed reflexively by the subject, such as in se baña (he bathes himself).
