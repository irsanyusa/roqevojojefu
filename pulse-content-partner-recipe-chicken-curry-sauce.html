    Recipe - Chicken Curry Sauce

    Servings: 4 Prep time: 15 minutes Cook time: 25mins ADVERTISEMENT Ingredients Chicken 4 cuts Irish potatoes, 4 wholes ADVERTISEMENT Green, red and yellow peppers Spring onions Olive/ vegetable oil.

    03 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Servings: 4

    Prep time: 15 minutes

    Cook time: 25mins

    ADVERTISEMENT

    Ingredients

    Chicken 4 cuts

    Irish potatoes, 4 wholes

    ADVERTISEMENT

    Green, red and yellow peppers

    Spring onions

    Olive/ vegetable oil.

    Preparation

    1)  Peel your Irish potatoes, blend two and chop two

    ADVERTISEMENT

    2) Wash and cut your chicken into cubes.

    3) Put the chopped chicken in a pot and add 3 cups of water.

    4.  Add chopped onions, spring onions, half a teaspoon of dried thyme, 2 teaspoons of curry powder, 3 chicken stock cubes and the blended potato to the pot.

    5) When the chicken starts boiling, add the remaining potato and 2 teaspoons of olive oil to the pot.

    6) Once the mixture boils, add the carrots and allow to cook for 5 minutes.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    7) Add the chopped peppers and cabbage.

    8) Stir and serve; healthy treat!

    Tip: I like the mix of my healthy vegetable chicken curry sauce with Pasta!
