    Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani have proved once again that they are #couplegoals. The pair made an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon via video chat. They are currently at Shelton’s ranch in Oklahoma and practicing self-isolating. But it was time for Shelton to get another haircut at the hands of Stefani. Watch the hilarious interview and haircut below.

    Blake Shelton Is Currently In Quarantine With A “Bunch Of Stefani’s”

    It seems like Shelton and Stefani have a full house. They are currently in isolation with Stefani’s sons Kingston, 13, Zuma, 11 and Apollo, 6. They have to find new ways to entertain themselves each day and giving Shelton haircuts is just one activity that they seem to do frequently. At one point you can see Stefani’s children’s reflections in the window as they watch their mother give Shelton a haircut. He asked Stefani to shave a ‘J’ and an ‘F’ into the sides of his head to pay homage to Jimmy Fallon. “I’m literally putting your initials in my head right now Jimmy I’m not kidding,” said the country singer. Stefani mentioned that the letters did not show up very well because of Shelton’s grey hair. “That looks terrible,” Stefani said giggling.

    This is not the first time Stefani has given Shelton a new do. She recently took him back to his roots and gave the country crooner a mullet. Stefani even shaved some lines into the side of Shelton’s head to give his mullet a little extra flare.

    Shelton had a mullet back in the early 2000s so it was certainly a fun throwback to the early days of his career. Some people even compared his new hair to that of Joe Exotic from the hit Netflix show Tiger King.

    Blake Shelton Spoke About Continuing To Film ‘The Voice’ Virtually

    Shelton also spoke about how they will probably have to continue filming this season of The Voice via video chat. The live shows are coming up in a few weeks and Shelton feels like Los Angeles will not be ready to host live events again by that time. He speaks about how crazy it will be to coach artists through a computer screen. He made it clear that he is not the most tech-savvy person. Stefani’s brother actually set up the computer that he was chatting on with Fallon which makes him question how he will be able to take on the task of filming the show remotely.

    SEE ALSO: ‘THE VOICE’ KNOCKOUTS:NICK JONAS’ EMBARRASSING REJECTION [VIDEO]

    The Voice is one of the many live shows that are in question about production amid the coronavirus outbreak. Much like American Idol, contestants need to perform live for audience votes. It will certainly be interesting to see how these reality talent competitions improvise and make it work. But with hilarious judges like Shelton, we’re sure the show will still be very entertaining.

    They Performed An Acoustic Version Of Their Hit Duet

    Shelton will surely have plenty of help when it comes to setting up equipment to film The Voice from the house full of Stefani’s. The cute couple even went on to perform an acoustic version of their hit duet “Nobody But You.” They sounded absolutely amazing and there is no denying the chemistry and love between these two. The official music video for the song has over 24 million views. Wow! Deservedly so, as the powerful lyrics depict a heartfelt love story.

    One comment on the video said “I’m not a country music fan but I love this song and these two people. Very humble and so down to earth. Please make more music together.” We completely agree. They are both super relatable people. Another person suggested that “they need to create an awards show for all of the wonderful ‘at home’ performances that have been taking place across the country and world.” How amazing would that be? These celebrities definitely deserve a round of applause for keeping fans entertained and putting smiles on our faces even during the most stressful and chaotic times.
