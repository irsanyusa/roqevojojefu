    "I can't believe its already been 3 months since the #SuperBowlLIV #Halftime Show! So much has happened since then, but I wanted to share a fun peek behind the scenes at our first rehearsal with the dancers and @Shakira ," she wrote in the caption.

    The videos show cool and sweet moments, like J.Lo getting her jam on with a bunch of little kids or just doing her usual fierce J.Lo ~thing~. And, of course, there are videos of J.Lo practicing with Shakira. (Tbh, J.Lo brings it with every performancethe only difference between rehearsal and the real deal was costumes and funky lighting.)
